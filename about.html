ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Kyle Wadas, a Computer Science undergrad at Neumont College of Computer Science.

Born in Alaska and raised in rural Idaho, I was lucky enough to find my love for art and coding despite the large agricultural setting around me.

Since enrolling at Neumont, I've participated and placed in three Game Jam events, and have been on the President's List for academic excellence ever since starting college in 2022. I currently hold a 3.8 GPA and am on track to graduate with a Bachelor's in Computer Science in September, 2025.

I've primarily focused on expressing my artistic ideas through game development. My more popular examples would be my work as lead artist for Game Jam projects like BIG BLAST OF ULTIMATE MUSCLE, BLOOD-DRIVE, and Spectres and Spirits, all of which can be found in my portfolio.

I've also delved into voice acting, which you can hear a bit of in ARMORED CANNIBAL. I'm always looking for new ways to express my creativity!
