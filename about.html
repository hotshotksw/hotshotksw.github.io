ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Kyle Wadas, a Computer Science undergrad at Neumont College of Computer Science.

Born in Alaska and raised in rural Idaho, I was lucky enough to find my love for art and coding

despite the large agricultural setting around me. Since enrolling at Neumont, I've participated

and placed in three Game Jam events, and have been on the President's List for academic

excellence ever since starting college in 2023.

I've primarily focused on expressing my artistic ideas through game development. My more-

popular examples would be my work as lead artist for Game Jam projects like BIG BLAST

OF ULTIMATE MUSCLE, BLOOD-DRIVE, and Spectres and Spirits, all of which can be

found in my portfolio. I've also delved into voice acting, though none of those projects have

been released just yet. I'm always looking for new ways to express my creativity!
